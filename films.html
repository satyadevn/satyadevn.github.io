Films

A list of films I've seen recently, and like.

The films are listed roughly in reverse chronological order in which I've seen them.

  1. The Intruder (1962), starring William Shatner, about a rabble-rouser fighting integration.
  2. Night of the Hunter (1955), directed by Charles Laughton (who played the lawyer in "Witness for the Prosecution"), and starring Robert Mitchum as a creepy "preacher"
  3. Solyaris, by Andrei Tarkovsky. One of my favorite movies of all times - it is impossible to describe why. But the hypnotic long shots and the dreamy Bach composition subtly modified by Eduard Artemyev has a lot to do with it. It seems Lem was furious with this movie - he should be, it is far superior to his novel.
  4. Mississippi Burning (1988)
